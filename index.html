What's Beyond?

Once upon a time there was a woman who lived in the mountains at the edge of the world. Every day this woman looked over the edge and wondered what was beyond.

The edge of the world was a lonely place, but the woman had a big brown bear to keep her company.

A mailman would also occasionally visit with news and packages of dehydrated food and sometimes even a rare piece of mail.

Today the mailman came with his deliveries and all he found was a note.
